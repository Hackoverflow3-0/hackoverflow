About

Chandigarh University presents Hackoverflow 2.0 , a two day hackathon under CU Technical Fest TECH INVENT '20 which intents to provide a plethora of opportunities and robust platform to the tech savvys and 'out of the box' thinkers from all over India to collaborate and draw together their technical capabilities and amazing ideas with a mindset of finding the solutions to the already existing problems and to build something innovative and exemplary. We are determined to identify and reward India's most promising tech prowess and nurture their technical skills. The hackathon is going to conduct in two phases: ONLINE PHASE - 4 March to 25 March , 2020 ** Only the best 45 ideas from across India will be called to attend the second phase. OFFLINE PHASE - 5 April - 6 April , 2020
